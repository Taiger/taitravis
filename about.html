A little about Tai. 

Tai has a Bachelors of Science from the Evergreen State College in Olympia, Washington, where he studied natural science, art and communication technology.

Tai was born and raised in California, and spent many of his formative years living in Asia. He has two fantastic children who are 11 and 8. His son wants to be a rocket engineer when he grows up and his daughter wants to be a ballerina.

Outside of art and technology, his passions include traveling and snowboarding. He is a currently a freelance contractor building unique websites. He has a strong preference to using Drupal.

He especially enjoys writing about himself in the third person!
